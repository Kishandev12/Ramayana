About This Project

This website provides an accurate translation of the Ramayana by Tulsidas, ensuring authenticity and clarity.

Translation

Here, you will find the translated verses of Ramcharitmanas.

Example Verse:

"श्री गुरु चरण सरोज रज, निज मनु मुकुरु सुधारि। बरनउँ रघुबर बिमल जसु, जो दायकु फल चारि॥"

Translation: "With the dust of the Guru's lotus feet, I cleanse the mirror of my mind and sing the pure glories of Lord Ram, which bestow the fourfold fruits of life."
